Methodology

Overview

This diagram outlines our process for analyzing political tone and media coverage in Spanish TV news programs. It includes data collection, audio extraction, transcription, and topic modeling, followed by tone classification using advanced natural language models.

Data Collection and Processing Steps

- Data Collection: We gather data from TV channels such as TVE, Antena 3, laSexta, and Telecinco, stored as MP4 video files.
- Audio Extraction: Using FFmpeg, the audio is extracted into WAV format.
- Speech-to-Text: The audio is transcribed to text.
- Topic Modeling: Using BERTopic, we identify major topics from the transcribed speech.
- Tone Classification: With models like GPT, we classify political tone (-1 for negative, 0 for neutral, and 1 for positive).
